How can we help you today?

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about our courses, mentorship, and training programs.

EBAL Innotech is a specialized educational platform designed to empower Mechanical, Electronics, Mechatronics, and Manufacturing engineering graduates. We bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements through practical training, advanced AR/VR technology, and expert mentorship.

We offer a flexible hybrid learning model. Many of our core interactive sessions and mentorship programs are conducted live online, while our specialized AR/VR and hands-on industry competent modules may include in-person practical sessions at our training facilities.

Yes! Upon successful completion of any of our core modules (such as Industry Foundation, VAVE, or Innovation), you will receive an industry-recognized certificate that you can add to your resume and professional profiles to showcase your new skills to employers.

Our AR/VR modules provide an immersive learning environment where you can interact with virtual machinery and complex engineering systems safely. Once enrolled in a relevant course, you will be provided with specific instructions and platform access details to utilize these advanced training tools.

Enrolling is simple. You can click on the "Register" button in our top navigation bar, which will direct you to our dedicated enrollment portal (TagMango). From there, you can view available batches, select your desired course, and complete the secure registration process.
