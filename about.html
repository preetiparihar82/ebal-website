Our Story

Bridging the gap between Academia and Industry

eBal Innotech is more than just an educational platform; we are an Industry Skill Accelerator. We recognized that while universities build theoretical foundations, the modern industrial landscape demands hands-on, practical expertise.

We deliver experiential training in Mechanical, Mechatronics, and Manufacturing domains by leveraging next-generation technologies like AI, AR/VR, and Additive Manufacturing. Our goal is simple: to transform dedicated graduates into elite, industry-ready professionals.
